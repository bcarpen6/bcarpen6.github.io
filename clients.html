Artists / Writers / Producers

Cameron Walker

Cameron Walker is an artist and songwriter from Southport, CT living in Los Angeles, CA. In 2012 he signed a publishing deal with SONGS Music Publishing. In 2014, Cameron was signed by David Hodges to his publishing company Third and Verse. Since then Cameron has written songs for artists such as All Time Low, Sabrina Carpenter, and The Ready Set. Cameron has also has written music for TV shows like Shameless and for many national brands such as Apple, T Mobile, and many others.


The Score

The Score is an American indie pop band that originated in Los Angeles, California. The group is a duo consisting of members Eddie Anthony (vocals, guitar) and Edan Dover (keyboard, producer). The Score were signed to Republic Records after their song "Oh My Love" was used by Asda for an advertising campaign while they were still an unsigned band in 2015. Their debut EP, Where Do You Run was released on September 25, 2015. On July 15th 2016, The Score released their new track "Unstoppable".


Denny White

Denny White is a writer, producer, and artist from Helmet, CA living in Los Angeles, CA. Denny is signed to Warner Chappell and has worked with artists ranging from Tiesto to The Fray. He has also written music for companies such as Toyota and films such as Netflix's XOXO that dropped this summer.


The Ready Set

The Ready Set is an artist and songwriter from Fort Wayne, IN living in Los Angeles, CA. Known for his songs "Love Like Woe" and "Give Me Your Hand" - Jordan spent the past five plus years releasing music on Warner Bros Records and touring the world. Jordan is published by Sony/ATV and splits his time between writing and touring with The Ready Set and writing and producing songs for TV/Film.


KARRA

KARRA is an artist and songwriter from Manasquan, NJ living in Los Angeles, CA. She start penning and performing top lines for electronic dance music artists on releases through prestigious labels like Armada, Dim Mak, Monstercat, and Spinnin' Records. She is self-published.


Sophia Kameron

Sophia Kameron is an artist and songwriter from Phoenix, AZ living in Los Angeles, CA. At just 15 years of age, Sophia released her debut single, Connected, which was produced by Steven Solomon (Sabrina Carpenter, Christina Perri, etc.). Sophia will release her debut EP later this year. She is self-published.


Paul Layton

Paul Layton is a songwriter, producer and multi-instrumentalist from Newport Beach, CA living in Los Angeles. He is published by SONGS Music Publishing, he has worked with Jai Waetford to TOKI-Monsta.


Michael Maughan

Michael Maughan is a writer and producer from Orange, CA living in Los Angeles, CA. Michael has recently been working with artists such as Traces and CANVAS while writing music and composing music for companies such as EA Sports to FedEX. Michael is self-published.
